About Us

Welcome to the world of digital adventures and cybersecurity challenges

Our Mission

At Hacker Escape Rooms, we're passionate about making cybersecurity education engaging and accessible. Our mission is to create immersive, challenging experiences that help people develop real-world security skills while having fun.

Who We Are

We are a team of cybersecurity professionals, educators, and game designers who believe that the best way to learn is through hands-on experience. Our diverse backgrounds in penetration testing, software development, and education allow us to create challenges that are both realistic and educational.

What We Do

We design and develop interactive escape room experiences that simulate real-world cybersecurity scenarios. From web application vulnerabilities to cryptographic puzzles, our challenges cover a wide range of security topics suitable for beginners to advanced practitioners.
